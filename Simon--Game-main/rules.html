Simon Game

Rules

1) Get ready to watch, remember, repeat!

2)The Simon game is the exciting game of lights and sounds.

3)At the beginning of the game, one of the 4 keys lights up randomly producing simultaneously a sound associated to the key.

4)So , the player needs to press the same color pad and on pressing the correct color pads in correct order level increases.

5)As level increases every time system adds new color to pattern.

6) So by keeping in mind the previous pattern and new color added a player have to follow the same pattern and level goes on increasing .

7) If player chooses wrong pattern the game end and player needs to restart it .

It's fast-paced play, with lights and sounds that can challenge you

Experience the fun as you repeat the patterns and advance to higher levels. Keep track of your score as you challenge friends or try to beat your own high score.
